About Us

Chitchat provides personalized skype classes and online video courses, to enable international students and business professionals improve their English skills for work.

Moumita Sen

Founder

Chitchat was started by Moumita in 2012. Moumita is from Kolkata (India) but has been living in San Francisco (USA) for the past few years. She has an MBA specializing in Marketing from Arizona, USA and has worked as a Consultant and Marketing Executive in Singapore, India and USA. An entrepreneur at heart, she ran her own business in high-end jewelry for five years, before starting Chitchat. Given her strong business background, she goes beyond the books and is able to provide great insights into the real business world.

Currently Chitchat has students from all over the world - USA, Brazil, Argentina, Mexico, Chile, Japan, Korea and France, just to name a few.
