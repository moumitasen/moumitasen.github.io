Classes

All classes are held online on Skype and are personalized to your requirements.

Spoken English Conversation- Basic / Intermediate / Advanced

  • Practice conversations based on social issues, news, movies or other topics of your interest.
  • Increase vocabulary and improve pronunciation, with a focus on your social setting and requirement.
  • Practice grammar to improve your common mistakes and learn frequently used idioms and phrases.

Business English

  • Learn commonly used words and phrases in business or office scenario.
  • Practice speaking using the business lingo in your area of specialization (Marketing, Finance, Design etc).
  • Prepare yourself for presentations, meetings or any other areas that you neeed to face on a regular basis.

Practice Interviews

  • Prepare for an upcoming interview by doing practice sessions that closely resemble actual interviews.
  • Get feedback on your performance and tips on how to improve your speaking skills in an interview.
  • Work on perfecting your resume and using the right words to describe your skills and experience.

Travel English for Tourists

  • Improve your English speaking skills before travelling to an English-speaking country.
  • Enjoy your vacation by being comfortable in having conversations required in typical travel situations.
  • Topics include transportation, money, restaurants, shopping, sightseeing plus any others of your choice.

Fun with Grammar

For those looking to focus on grammar and speak impeccable English, I will go deeper into the rules with examples through conversations. Don't worry, it won't be as dry as it sounds- you won't even realize that it's grammar!

Foreign students in College

I will work towards improving your listening, comprehension and expression skills, using material from your course and outside. For those taking an English course from a language school, I will focus on conversation practice to make you more fluent and confident. I can also help you prepare for TOEFL if you are intending to take the test.
