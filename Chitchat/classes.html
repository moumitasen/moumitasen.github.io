Classes

All classes are held online on Skype

Classes have to be a minimum duration of 1 hour

All classes are flexible and personalized to focus on what is important for you.

English Conversation- Basic/ Intermediate/ Advanced

I will work with you on different areas starting with grammar, listening skills, comprehension readings/videos, role play conversations based on situations, basic vocabulary, pronunciation, idioms and phrases and a lot more.

Business English
The focus will be to better equip you for presentations, speeches, meetings, writing reports or any specific help you might need. I will help you learn the business lingo and key words/phrases. If you run your own business and need help with marketing materials, given my marketing/branding background I can help you with that too.   

English for Travelers
This beginning level course is a great way to combine learning English and a vacation. It is designed to enhance your travel enjoyment by making you comfortable in carrying on conversations required in typical travel situations- transportation, money, restaurants, shopping, sightseeing and more.

Fun with Grammar
For those looking to focus on grammar and speak impeccable English, I will go deeper into the rules with examples through conversations. Don't worry, it won't be as dry as it sounds- you won't even realize that it's grammar!

Foreign students in College
I will work towards improving your listening, comprehension and expression skills, using material from your course and outside. For those taking an English course from a language school, I will focus on conversation practice to make you more fluent and confident. I can also help you prepare for TOEFL if you are intending to take the test.
