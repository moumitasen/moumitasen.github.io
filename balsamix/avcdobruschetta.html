Avocado Bacon Bruschetta

Lightly toasted bread topped with sliced avocados, bacon and balsamic vinegar.

Posted on May 31, 2013

Ingredients: 4 slices of whole-wheat baguette 1 ripe avocado 2 slices of bacon 3 tablespoons bacon fat 2 tablespoons Modena balsamic vinegar Method: 1. Fry the bacon on a pan on medium heat for about 5 minutes. It should be well-done but not crispy. Slice the bacon into thin strips. Reserve the oil from the bacon. 2. Toast the bread in the oven for 2 minutes. Drizzle 1/2 tablespoon of reserved bacon fat on toasted bread. 3. Slice the avocado vertically and put about 4 each on the bread. 4. Put about bacon strips on top of the avocado. 5. Drizzle will remaining bacon fat and avocado. 6. Serve immediately. Notes: Cooking bacon Balsamic vinegar
