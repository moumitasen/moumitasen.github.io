Moumita Sen

Moumita is from Kolkata (India) but has been living in San Francisco (USA) for the past few years. She has an MBA specializing in Marketing from Arizona, USA and has worked as a Consultant and Marketing Executive in Singapore, India and USA. An entrepreneur at heart, she ran her own business in high-end jewelry for five years, before starting Chitchat. Given her strong business background, she focuses on conducting Chitchat's Business English Classes and Job Interview Practice Sessions.

Lisa Baughn

Lisa lives in Colorado (USA) but believes herself to be a citizen of the world, having traveled to twelve countries. She has taught high-school English as a Fulbright English Teaching Assistant in Indonesia. She has a Master's in Geography as well as a TEFL (Teaching English as a Foreign Language) Certification from 'The Language House' in Prague. She looks forward to meeting new students and taking upon the challenge of improving their English while helping them attain their learning goals.
